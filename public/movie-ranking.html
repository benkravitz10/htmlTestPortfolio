My top 3 movies!

Because I said so...

Spaceballs

It's just too damn funny. Everything about it, especially since I don't like Star Wars.

Grown Ups 1 & 2

Both of theses were hysterical, with the perfect cast. The 2nd was comedy gold, with Shaq being afraid of his older, yet shorter brother. Meanwhile, Shaq towered over him.

The Super Mario Movie

It lived up to everything I wanted in a Super Mario Bros Movie. There were so many references to the games. I loved it.
